How Babbel Books tailors
your child's story

Every book we create is shaped around your child's age — from the words we use to the illustrations we make. Here's how it works.

🌙

Ages 0 – 2

Young Tots

Simple, soothing stories

Short sentences, gentle repetition, and warm emotional moments — perfect for reading aloud at bedtime.

Bright, bold illustrations

High-contrast colours, large simple shapes, and a single character per page — easy for young eyes to follow.

A keepsake from day one

Even toddlers have stories in them. We capture that first precious voice and turn it into something they'll grow up reading.

✨

Ages 3 – 5

Young Readers

Imaginative, character-driven stories

A real beginning, middle, and end — with heroes, gentle adventures, and satisfying resolutions that feel magical.

Warm, expressive illustrations

Painterly watercolour spreads with expressive faces, cosy environments, and a gently magical atmosphere.

Friends and toys, written in

Tell us their best friend's name or favourite stuffy — we'll write them into the story as real characters.

🚀

Ages 6 – 10

Post-Toddler Readers

More adventurous stories

A structured plot with real stakes, humour, clever twists, and a hero who earns their victory — every time.

Richer scenes and detail

Dynamic compositions with world-building, bold colour, and characters full of personality — illustrations that reward a long look.

Illustrations that fuel imagination

Art that goes beyond the words on the page — inviting older kids to look closer, imagine more, and read it again.
