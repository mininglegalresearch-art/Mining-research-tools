Prompt A — Plain Cover 
PROMPT A — PLAIN COVER SHOT
[Paste into your image generator · Set to 16:9]

Professional product photography of a plain hardcover children's book, centered in frame,
shot from a 3/4 elevated angle. Clean white background (#FFFFFF), soft studio lighting
with subtle shadows beneath the book.

The book is closed. Its cover is deliberately unassuming: a simple cream-beige linen
texture, slightly worn at the corners. The only element on the cover is the title
"BABBEL BOOKS" embossed or printed in a clean, modest serif typeface centered on the
front — subtle, not decorative, as if stamped there plainly. The spine shows faint
horizontal cloth ribbing. The pages are thick and slightly yellowed at the edges,
giving a sense of age and quiet mystery. Nothing else about the exterior hints at
what is inside.

The mood is deliberately understated — like something you might overlook on a shelf,
yet somehow feel drawn to pick up. Subtle warm shadows beneath. No text, no labels,
no branding, no other objects in frame.

Photorealistic rendering, 16:9 aspect ratio, product catalog quality.
Shot on Phase One IQ4 150MP, 120mm macro lens, f/8, studio strobe lighting with large
softbox above and white bounce cards on sides. Ultra-sharp detail, 8K quality
downsampled to 4K.
Prompt B — Magic Burst 
PROMPT B — MAGIC BURST SHOT (THE OPENED BOOK)
[Paste into your image generator · Set to 16:9 · Reference Prompt A for consistency]

The same plain children's book from Prompt A, now dramatically opened at its center,
lying flat on a white background (#FFFFFF). From within its pages, an explosion of
magical wonder erupts outward in a dramatic freeze-frame.

Bursting from the open pages in a spectacular radial explosion:
- A magnificent golden dragon mid-roar, scales glinting, wings fully spread
- A tiny fairy with iridescent wings trailing pixie dust spirals
- An enchanted forest of luminescent blue-green trees growing upward from the pages
- A crescent moon and cascade of glowing stars scattering across the white background
- A brave young child hero in a red cape, leaping outward from the book
- Giant luminescent mushrooms in purples and teals erupting from the lower pages
- A friendly oversized whale breaching through golden light beams
- Cascading rainbow ribbons of color streaming outward like ink spilling free
- Swirls of glowing magical script — ancient letters and symbols floating in the air
- Flower petals in hot pink, electric yellow, and deep violet spiraling outward
- A small castle turret emerging from the top-left page, banners waving
- Dozens of tiny birds in flight, each leaving a trail of colored light

The overall explosion follows a strong radial composition — everything erupting from
the center crease of the book outward toward the frame edges. The white background
makes every vibrant color pop with maximum contrast. The book itself remains intact
and recognizable — the plain, humble cover visible on either side — making the
contrast between exterior plainness and interior magic the visual punch.

Photorealistic mixed with painterly magic illustration style. Soft studio lighting
on the book itself, magical interior glow emanating from the pages. Same white
background as Prompt A for visual continuity. 16:9 aspect ratio, ultra-detailed,
8K quality. High-speed freeze-frame photography aesthetic — every particle sharp.
Prompt C — Video Transition 
PROMPT C — VIDEO TRANSITION
[Paste into your video model · Upload Prompt A output as start frame · Upload Prompt B output as end frame]

START FRAME: A plain, closed hardcover children's book sitting centered on a pure white
background. Soft studio lighting. Unassuming, humble, quiet — a cream linen cover with
no illustration. Completely still.

END FRAME: The same book now fully opened flat, with a spectacular radial explosion of
magical characters, creatures, colors, and light erupting from its pages — dragon, fairy,
enchanted forest, glowing stars, rainbow ribbons, castle, birds with light trails,
luminescent mushrooms — all frozen mid-burst against the white background.

TRANSITION: The book sits completely still for 0.8 seconds. A faint golden glow begins
to pulse from the spine — barely visible, like light leaking from inside. The cover
slowly begins to open from right to left with a smooth, weighty motion (1 second),
the way an old hardcover feels when you crack it open. As the cover reaches halfway
open, the light from inside intensifies dramatically.

The moment the cover reaches fully open — an instant, explosive burst. All magical
elements erupt simultaneously outward from the center crease in a radial shockwave.
Each element — dragon, fairy, trees, stars, characters — launches outward along its
own path with a slight cascading delay (staggered by 0.1–0.2 seconds) so the explosion
feels layered and rich rather than instantaneous. Color floods the frame in waves.
The final arrangement holds for 1.5 seconds.

STYLE: Photorealistic book, magical painterly explosion. White background throughout.
No camera movement — locked-off overhead/3-quarter angle. The only motion is the book
opening and the magic erupting. Warm golden light pulses from the book's center as
the source of all magic.

MOOD: The quiet before the storm — then pure wonder. Like the feeling of opening a
book as a child and being completely transported.

DURATION: 4–5 seconds total.
ASPECT RATIO: 16:9
QUALITY: High fidelity, smooth 24fps minimum, no artifacts.

VARIATIONS TO CONSIDER:
- Reverse: Start with magic fully erupted, have it all rush back into the closing book
- Loop: Close → glow builds → open → burst → hold → close again (seamless)
- Slow-mo: Extend opening and burst to 8–10 seconds for ultra-dramatic effect
